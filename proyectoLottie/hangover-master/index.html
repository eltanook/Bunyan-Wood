About us

We are...

We are Bunyan Wood, a team of lumberjacks whose main objective is to bring back to the community the custom of choosing to use wood, offering our top quality furniture or firewood.

Our wood...

Our wood is cut by hand by each one of us, leaving a very satisfactory result, this being a combination between rusticity and quality, resulting in the perfect firewood, or in a totally unique piece of furniture.

Come visit us...

Come visit us and see how we do our work. You will be able to contemplate that we do not threaten nature, and to see the beautiful natural landscape that surrounds our work area.
